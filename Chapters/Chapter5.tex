\chapter {Pong as a Testbed}
\label{chap:pong}
Pong provides a controlled setting in which to compare the two paradigms developed in the preceding chapters. The environment is complex enough to require learning visual representations from raw pixels, yet simple enough that both \acrshort{dqn} and \acrshort{a2c} can be trained to competent play on a single GPU within a reasonable time budget. This chapter formalizes Pong as a Markov decision process, describes the preprocessing pipeline that transforms raw frames into a suitable state representation, and presents the implementation and training results for each method.
